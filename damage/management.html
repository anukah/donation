Faculty of Management

Severe damage to one of the University of Peradeniya’s key academic faculties, disrupting teaching, learning, and administrative operations.

Overview

The Faculty of Management experienced extensive flooding during the Ditwah cyclone, affecting academic buildings, ICT infrastructure, and essential facilities. Thousands of students and staff were impacted as lecture delivery, digital learning, and administrative services were brought to a halt.

Damage to Buildings

  • • Lecture halls and classrooms flooded
  • • Library facilities damaged
  • • Auditorium infrastructure affected
  • • Student cafeteria impacted
  • • Administrative and staff office spaces inundated

Other damaged equipment: Freezers, air-conditioning units, speaker systems, and auxiliary electronic devices.

Infrastructure Damage

Electrical systems, internal network cabling, and internet connectivity were severely affected. Structural elements including retaining walls and internal access routes were compromised, posing safety risks and delaying reopening.

Impact on Education

Teaching schedules were interrupted due to unusable lecture spaces and loss of ICT facilities. Online and distance learning platforms were disrupted, resulting in postponed assessments, delayed coursework, and academic progression challenges.
