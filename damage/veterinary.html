FACULTY OF VETERINARY MEDICINE

HOME TO SRI LANKA'S ONLY VETERINARY TEACHING HOSPITAL

Overview

A Nationally Critical Institution

The Faculty of Veterinary Medicine houses Sri Lanka's only Veterinary Teaching Hospital, playing a vital role in animal healthcare, education, research, and national disease surveillance. Damage to this facility impacts the entire country.

The loss of this facility affects veterinary education, research, and animal healthcare nationwide.

Damage Report

Affected Facilities

Teaching Hospital Damage

  • • Theatre tables
  • • Anaesthetic machines
  • • Surgical instruments
  • • Patient monitors
  • • Sterilisation units
  • • Biosafety cabinets

Neonatal care units and necropsy facilities were also severely affected, limiting critical veterinary training and lifesaving procedures.

Farm Animal Hospital

The Farm Animal Hospital sustained extensive structural and equipment damage, disrupting services essential for large animal treatment, field training, and rural livestock support.

Biochemistry Laboratory

Key laboratory equipment was damaged, affecting teaching, research, and diagnostic services critical to veterinary education.

CAADDR – Aquatic Disease Centre

  • • Biosafety cabinets
  • • PCR machines
  • • Incubators
  • • Microscopes
  • • Autoclaves

Damage to CAADDR has significantly affected Sri Lanka's national aquatic animal disease diagnostic capacity, posing risks to food security and export industries.
